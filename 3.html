WHY IS HE FAT?

The usual reason given for people getting fat is that they eat too much and/or exercise too little Some people are destined to gain weight because they have inherited fat genes from their parents. High caloric foods seem to be inherently more attractive than other foods for reasons that probably have to do with survival in conditions that human beings found themselves in most of the time throughout our history. But no longer.
